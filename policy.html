Privacy Policy & Terms

Last updated: March 2026

Welcome to Huy Sài Gòn's website. By using this site or submitting a reservation, you agree to the terms outlined below. We've written these in plain language — no legal jargon.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our booking form, we may collect:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Reservation details (date, time, party size, seating preference)
  • Dietary notes and special requests you provide
  • Any photos you voluntarily upload
  • How you heard about us (optional)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Confirm and manage your table reservation
  • Send you booking confirmation via email or phone
  • Personalise your in-café experience where applicable
  • Send newsletters if you opted in (you can unsubscribe anytime)

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third-party advertisers or data brokers.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers within Vietnam, compliant with the Personal Data Protection Decree (No. 13/2023/NĐ-CP). Reservation data is retained for 12 months, after which it is permanently deleted unless you have an active membership account.

4. Cookies

This website uses only essential functional cookies (e.g., maintaining navigation state). We do not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party analytics.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for newsletter communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the relevant Vietnamese authority

To exercise these rights, email us at privacy@huysaigon.vn.

6. External Links

Our website links to external services including Google Maps and YouTube. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these platforms — please review their own policies.

7. Image Credits

All photography on this website sourced from Unsplash under the Unsplash License. Icons via fav.farm (emoji favicons, free to use).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Questions? Email hello@huysaigon.vn or visit us at 19/7 Nguyễn Chí Thanh, Quận 1.
