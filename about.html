Our Story

From a single Phin filter to a city institution.

Born from a love of real coffee

Rang Sài Gòn was founded in 2019 by Minh and Lan, two Saigonese who spent years travelling — from Hanoi to Hội An, from Melbourne to Tokyo — studying how great coffee is made. They came home with one mission: bring that craft back to the streets of HCMC.

We source our green beans directly from small-holder farmers in Đà Lạt, Buôn Ma Thuột, and the Central Highlands. We roast in-house every Thursday, so what you drink is never more than a week off the roaster.

Our space on Nguyễn Trãi was designed to be a pause — somewhere between a library and a living room. Come for the coffee. Stay for the quiet.

What We Stand For

Three principles guide every decision we make.

🌱 Direct Trade

We pay above fair-trade price for every batch, building long-term relationships with our farmers in the highlands.

🔥 Fresh Roasts

Our in-house roaster runs every Thursday. Same-week freshness, always. No warehouse shelf dust.

♻️ Zero-Waste Goals

Used coffee grounds become compost, cups are returnable, and our straws are bamboo — not plastic.

📚 Community Space

Free Wi-Fi, laptop-friendly tables, and monthly cupping events open to anyone curious about coffee.
